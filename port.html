Cyber Security Analyst!

I conduct in-depth investigations into suspicious activities or vulnerabilities. This involves analyzing logs, network traffic, and system configurations to identify potential weaknesses or signs of malicious activity. Sometimes, I collaborate with other teams such as IT or incident response to gather more information or coordinate a response. One of the most rewarding aspects of my job is devising and implementing security measures to mitigate risks. Whether it's updating firewall rules, patching software vulnerabilities, or conducting security awareness training for employees, every action I take helps strengthen our defenses against cyber threats. However, the world of cyber security is always evolving, and staying ahead of the curve requires constant learning and adaptation. I dedicate time each week to research emerging threats, new attack techniques, and the latest security tools and technologies. This knowledge not only helps me better protect our organization but also enables me to anticipate and preempt future threats.
